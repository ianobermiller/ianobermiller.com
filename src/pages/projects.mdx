## Mobile

### Legends of Descent

[<img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-104" title="lod-logo" src="https://i1.wp.com/ianobermiller.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/06/logo.png?resize=480%2C250" alt="Legends of Descent Logo" width="480" height="250" srcset="https://i1.wp.com/ianobermiller.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/06/logo.png?w=480 480w, https://i1.wp.com/ianobermiller.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/06/logo.png?resize=300%2C156 300w" sizes="(max-width: 480px) 100vw, 480px" data-recalc-dims="1" />][2]

- Dungeon crawler style RPG for Windows Phone
- Unlimited procedurally generated dungeon maps, items,  and enemy groups
- Slay monsters, reap loot, and equip yourself with the very best weapons and armor
- My favorite pieces: dungeon map generation, particle effects
- Written in C# using the XNA framework
- <http://legendsofdescent.com>

[<img class="alignnone wp-image-234" src="https://i2.wp.com/ianobermiller.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/06/Screen-Shot-2014-09-06-at-8.58.26-AM.png?resize=600%2C378" alt="NuclearMail" width="600" height="378" srcset="https://i2.wp.com/ianobermiller.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/06/Screen-Shot-2014-09-06-at-8.58.26-AM.png?resize=1024%2C645 1024w, https://i2.wp.com/ianobermiller.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/06/Screen-Shot-2014-09-06-at-8.58.26-AM.png?resize=300%2C189 300w, https://i2.wp.com/ianobermiller.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/06/Screen-Shot-2014-09-06-at-8.58.26-AM.png?w=2000 2000w" sizes="(max-width: 600px) 100vw, 600px" data-recalc-dims="1" />][3]

- Code at: <https://github.com/ianobermiller/nuclearmail>/
- Demo at: <http://ianobermiller.github.io/nuclearmail/>
